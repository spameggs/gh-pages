PAYTPV plugin offers you a functionality to work with payment gateway PAYTPV, which allows processing credit and debit cards.

PAYTPV is a payment system that helps you to collect and process payments made by Visa and Mastercard credit/debit cards through a secured system of the payment provider on your website. It offers a secured transaction process along with anti-fraud solutions, statistics with transactions at the specially created management interface.
The payments can be done in USD, JPY, GBP and EUR currencies.

*The limitation of this payment provider is: you must hold a European bank account and be an EU citizen.
