Bank Transfer plugin is the offline payment system that offers 2 ways for making payments at your classifieds website.

Pay by check - this payment option allows administrators to set own personal data for receiving cheques. The system creates a unique ID and displays it to a user for further use in the cheque. After receiving of the cheque with that ID the administrator will activate the service for the user.

Wire transfer - using this payment option a user can give his bank requisites and send them to the site administrator. After that the administrator will draw out needed amount of money from the user’s account and activate the service on the website.

This Flynax plugin is a convenient solution for classifieds websites oriented for the regions where payment systems are not highly developed and there are not enough online payment gateway alternatives. Bank Transfer Payment plugin will help you and your users to use your classifieds websites without difficulties.
