Massmailer/Newslletter plugin is a convenient tool for making massmails.

This Flynax plugin can help you to create a lot of massmails without difficulty and keep your site users, subscribers and those who contacted you throught the contact form about anything you want them to know.

You can use the edited texts and graphics in the text of your massmails. Your users may easily subscribe to newsletters or unsubscribe from massmails using a specially designed subscription block in the frontend of your classifieds website. Also users can unsubscribe from massmails right from letters if they click in Unsubscribe link.

Massmailer/Newsletter plugin sends message by message time after time with the help of AJAX thus decreases the chance to get into SPAM.

Compatible to Flynax 4 version.
