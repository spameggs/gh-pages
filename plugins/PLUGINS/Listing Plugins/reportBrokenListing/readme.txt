Flynax Report Broken Listing adds an option to report listings with not allowed or damaged content by your users and site visitors. Thus with this Flynax plugin you will be able to have your listings in right order.

The Report Broken Listing plugin adds special icons on a page with listings’ details into all grids with listings. Using these icons users may describe his report on a particular listing and send it to you. Further you as an administrator will decide whether the content damaged, not allowed or ok. Then you can delete or leave the listing as is. Also this plugin gives an opportunity to recall a report by a user in the case if he is not sure in it.

This Flynax plugin lets you manage the content of your website with the help and have it in the right order.
